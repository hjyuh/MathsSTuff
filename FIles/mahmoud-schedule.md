## Rules

1. **Math is the default.** Roblox building only happens when there's a task.
2. **Roblox coding is always background.** Claude Code runs while you do math.
3. **Morning block is non-negotiable.** This is your sharpest time.
4. **Trigger log happens daily.** Even 2 minutes. "I saw X → I did Y because Z."
5. **Insane days are for breaks and motivation peaks.** Not daily — you'll burn out.
6. **Prayer times are the skeleton.** The schedule shifts with them throughout the year.
